| 8 | Development Tracking | Growth Over Time | Fixed enum |
| 9 | Composite Readiness | Overall Signal | Fixed enum |

LOCKED INDICATOR LABELS:

| Section | Indicator Name | Locked Values |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| 1 | This Week | Strong Week / Uneven Week / Reset Week |
| 2 | Standout | [Agent-generated 3-5 word phrase] |
| 3 | Self-View | Grounded / Building / Finding Their Footing |
| 4 | Emotional Weight | Light / Noticeable / Heavier |
| 5 | Confidence | Growing / Steady / Fluctuating |
| 6 | Pattern | [Agent-generated 3-5 word phrase] |
| 7 | Awareness | Nothing of Note / Something to Note |
| 8 | Progress | Building Foundations / Getting Consistent / Strong Momentum |
| 9 | Overall | Good Week (Green) / Steady Week (Yellow) / Support Week (Orange) |

INDICATOR DETERMINATION RULES:

Section 1 — THIS WEEK:
  Growth tier → Strong Week
  Mixed tier → Uneven Week
  Reset tier → Reset Week

Section 3 — SELF-VIEW:
  Confidence Building + Aligned self-ratings → Grounded
  Confidence Building + no alignment data → Building
  Confidence Stable + Aligned → Grounded
  Confidence Stable + Conflated → Building
  Confidence Variable → Finding Their Footing
  Confidence Building + Undervalued → Building
  Default for early weeks or ambiguous → Building

Section 4 — EMOTIONAL WEIGHT:
  emotional_intensity = Low → Light
  emotional_intensity = Moderate → Noticeable
  emotional_intensity = High → Heavier

Section 5 — CONFIDENCE:
  Confidence momentum Building → Growing
  Confidence momentum Stable → Steady
  Confidence momentum Variable → Fluctuating

Section 7 — AWARENESS:
  No elevated risk + stress Low/Moderate + no derailer activation → Nothing of Note
  ANY of: Elevated stress, derailer activation, Low recommitment, pattern_shift_detected=Yes → Something to Note

Section 8 — PROGRESS:
  Growth phase Emerging or Developing → Building Foundations
  Growth phase Consistent → Getting Consistent
  Growth phase Leadership → Strong Momentum

Section 9 — OVERALL:
  Composite Readiness ↑ Positive (Green) → Good Week (Green)
  Composite Readiness → Steady (Yellow) → Steady Week (Yellow)
  Composite Readiness ↓ Attention (Orange) → Support Week (Orange)

Sections 2 and 6 — AGENT-GENERATED:
  3-5 word phrase in parent-understandable language. No framework terminology.

FORMAT RULES:
- Indicator line is structured data — use locked enum values exactly. Do NOT make conversational.
- Blurb is Coach Arron's voice — adds context to the indicator. Do NOT restate the indicator.
- WRONG: "This Week: Grace had a really strong week!" (conversational indicator)
- WRONG: "This Week: Strong Week\nThis was a strong week for Grace." (blurb restates indicator)
- CORRECT: "This Week: Strong Week\nGrace's daily routine held consistent all week."
