
VIRTUSFOCUS – COACH INSIGHTS COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK
(Operational Guardrails for Institutional Reporting)

============================================================
PURPOSE
============================================================

This document defines the compliance, institutional, and reporting guardrails
for the VirtusFocus Coach Insights Engine.

Coach Insights are designed to provide high-level performance indicators
related to consistency, focus, self-regulation, and habit stability.

They are NOT:
- Mental health evaluations
- Clinical assessments
- Therapy services
- Diagnostic tools
- Participation monitoring systems
- Coaching directives
- Selection or discipline mechanisms

Coach Insights function as informational performance signals only.


============================================================
CORE POSITIONING (INSTITUTIONAL SAFE LANGUAGE)
============================================================

VirtusFocus is a mental performance and leadership development system.

It supports consistency, focus, composure, and self-regulation in athletic performance.

It does not assess, diagnose, or treat mental health conditions and does not replace licensed mental health professionals or campus wellness services.

Coach Insights provide high-level performance indicators only.
They do not require staff to interpret mental health conditions or provide interventions.


============================================================
COACH ACCESS BOUNDARIES
============================================================

Coaches and staff may view:

- Trend-level performance indicators
- Weekly classification (Growth / Mixed / Reset)
- Consistency signals
- Focus distribution trends (Bullseye-based)
- Recommitment strength indicators
- Directional confidence trends
- Growth phase classification

Coaches may NOT view:

- Athlete-written journaling content
- Raw Bullseye entries
- Raw Motivation Inventory responses
- Daily scoring breakdowns
- Time spent in the system
- Completion frequency
- Usage history
- Personal disclosures

The system presents outcomes, not participation behavior.


============================================================
VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION PROTECTION
============================================================

The Coach Insights Engine is designed to preserve voluntary participation status.

The system:

- Does not assign tasks
- Does not track completion rates
- Does not report login frequency
- Does not report time spent
- Does not notify coaches of missed activity
- Does not create coach-directed follow-up

Coach visibility is limited to informational trend indicators only.

Insights are observational, not directive.


============================================================
PROHIBITED METRICS
============================================================

The following metrics must NEVER be generated or displayed:

- Time spent in app
- Streak counts
- Daily completion counts
- Last login timestamps
- Participation rankings
- Athlete comparisons by name
- “At-risk” labels tied to individuals
- Engagement scores
- Compliance or adherence ratings
- Effort-based metrics

No metric may imply discipline, enforcement, or evaluation of commitment.


============================================================
APPROVED METRIC TAXONOMY
============================================================

Metrics must be categorized as Signals or Indicators only.

Approved categories include:

1. Mental Readiness Indicator (Trend Only)
   - Directional (↑ ↓ →)
   - No raw daily values

2. Consistency Signal
   - Stable
   - Variable
   - Improving

3. Stress Load Band
   - Low
   - Moderate
   - Elevated
   (No source attribution)

4. Confidence Momentum (Directional)

5. Focus Distribution (Bullseye Trend)
   - Center-Dominant
   - Mixed Focus
   - Outer-Ring Drift Detected

6. Recommitment Strength
   - Strong
   - Moderate
   - Low

7. Growth Phase Status
   - Emerging
   - Developing
   - Consistent
   - Leadership

All metrics must be trend-based, non-punitive, and informational.


============================================================
LANGUAGE STANDARDS
============================================================

Use language such as:

- Indicator
- Signal
- Trend
- Directional
- Informational
- Context
- Pattern
- Stability
- Developmental phase

Avoid language such as:

- Compliance
- Adherence
- Monitoring
- Tracking behavior
- Accountability enforcement
- At-risk
- Discipline
- Required
- Evaluation metric

Language framing is critical to institutional compliance.


============================================================
PLAYING TIME & SELECTION PROTECTION
============================================================

All Coach Insights must include the following disclaimer:

"These indicators are informational only and may not be used as a basis for participation, selection, or disciplinary decisions."

Insights provide contextual awareness, not evaluative judgment.


============================================================
DATA & PRIVACY LIMITATIONS
============================================================

VirtusFocus does not collect:

- Clinical mental health data
- Medical records
- Diagnostic information

Coach Insights present performance-related patterns only.

No personal disclosures are displayed in reporting outputs.


============================================================
REPORTING PRINCIPLES
============================================================

Coach Insights must:

- Be concise
- Be structured
- Avoid emotional narrative
- Avoid athlete quotes
- Avoid personal detail
- Avoid interpretation beyond structured data
- Present trend summaries, not day-level breakdowns

The system must always err toward over-protection rather than over-reporting.


============================================================
FINAL COMPLIANCE STANDARD
============================================================

Coach Insights are designed to resemble:

- Wellness dashboards
- Readiness summaries
- High-level performance trend reports

They are not practice tools, instruction tools, or behavioral enforcement mechanisms.

The integrity of voluntary participation, privacy boundaries,
and institutional compliance must be preserved at all times.

End of Document.
