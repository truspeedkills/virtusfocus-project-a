Compliance-Approved Language Blocks
(Use these verbatim across decks, contracts, onboarding, and AD approvals)

1. Core Positioning Statement (Master Block)
VirtusFocus is a mental performance and leadership development system.
It is designed to support consistency, focus, composure, and self-regulation in athletic performance.
VirtusFocus is not a mental health treatment, therapy service, or diagnostic tool, and it does not replace licensed mental health professionals or campus wellness resources.

2. Scope of Use (Very Important for Compliance)
VirtusFocus focuses exclusively on performance habits, mindset structure, and athlete self-leadership.
It does not assess, diagnose, or treat mental health conditions.
Athletes are encouraged to utilize institutional mental health and wellness services for clinical or personal mental health concerns.

3. Athlete Participation Language
Participation in VirtusFocus supports personal responsibility, reflection, and performance consistency.
All engagement is athlete-driven and designed to promote independence, not reliance on staff or external intervention.

4. Relationship to Mental Health Services
VirtusFocus is designed to complement, not replace, existing mental health and wellness services.
The system provides structure and clarity that may reduce performance-related stress by improving focus, preparation, and response to pressure.
(This line is key—it positions VirtusFocus as preventative, not therapeutic.)

5. Data & Privacy Language (Light but Reassuring)
VirtusFocus provides performance-related engagement and consistency data.
It does not collect clinical mental health data, medical records, or diagnostic information.

6. Coach & Staff Role Clarity
Coaches and staff are provided high-level performance indicators related to engagement and consistency.
VirtusFocus does not require staff to interpret mental health conditions or provide mental health interventions.
