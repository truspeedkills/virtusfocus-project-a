VIRTUSFOCUS – PARENT-FACING SUMMARY FRAMEWORK

PURPOSE:
Provide supportive, growth-based update without technical detail.

TONE:
- Encouraging
- Professional
- Clear
- Non-clinical

STRUCTURE:

1) Growth Highlight
   - One paragraph on habit or leadership development.

2) Current Focus Area
   - One paragraph on behavioral improvement focus (non-technical).

3) Identity Development
   - Who the athlete is becoming (self-leadership lens).

4) How Parent Can Support
   - 1–3 short bullet suggestions.
   - Reinforce independence and ownership.

RULES:
- Do not mention numeric Win the Day scores.
- Do not mention internal scoring classifications.
- Keep length 250–400 words.
