THE BULLSEYE METHOD

The Bullseye Method
Visualize a target with six rings, each representing a different level of control:

________________________________________
Center Ring (1) – What You Can Control (Bullseye)
These are the things within your power, and this is where your energy should go.
• Your attitude
• Your words and actions
• Your breathing and body posture
• Your effort
• How you treat others
• The choices you make under pressure
• Staying aligned with your values
⟶ Goal: Focus here. These are the actions that move you toward the bullseye and reduce stress long-term.
________________________________________
Middle Ring (2-3) – What You Can Influence
These are things you can't control directly, but you can affect to some degree.
• Other people’s behavior or mindset (through modeling, feedback, communication)
• Team culture (through leadership)
• The outcome of a situation (based on your input/effort)
• Timing of events (sometimes)
⟶ Goal: Acknowledge your influence but don’t fixate on the outcome.
________________________________________
Outer Ring (4-6) – What You Can’t Control
These are fully outside your power and often the source of unnecessary stress.
• Other people’s decisions
• The past
• The future
• Weather, traffic, delays
• Whether someone likes or dislikes you
• Unexpected events
⟶ Goal: Let go. Worrying about these moves you away from the bullseye and drains your energy.
________________________________________
How to Use It in Stressful Situations
1. Pause and Ask:
“Am I focusing on something I can control, influence, or can’t control at all?”
2. Recenter on the Bullseye:
“What action can I take right now that aligns with my values and focuses on what I control?”
3. Act with Intention:
Choose a response (not a reaction) based on what’s inside the bullseye: your effort, your mindset, your behavior.

You will use the Bullseye Method to evaluate your day's/week’s events in your Weekly Review. Learning to apply this method to all aspects of your life makes even the most difficult situations – easy to navigate!
