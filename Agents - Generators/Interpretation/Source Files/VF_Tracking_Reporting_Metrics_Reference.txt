VF Tracking & Reporting Metrics Reference

Win the Day Weekly Interpretation:
28–35 (Growth Week) – Strong routine consistency and self-leadership.
14–27 (Mixed Week) – Inconsistent routine execution.
0–13 (Reset Week) – Significant inconsistency; recommitment required.

Daily Category Meaning:
4–5 Points – Win the Day (strong execution).
2–3 Points – Neutral Day (partial follow-through).
0–1 Points – Missed the Mark (routine breakdown).

Coach-Safe Reporting Indicators:
Engagement consistency (Win/Neutral/Missed distribution).
Recommitment signal (response after missed day).
Routine stability (range of category distribution).
Controllable focus quality (Bullseye usage).

Compliance Reminder:
All reporting must remain performance-based and non-clinical.
Do not include psychological diagnoses or mental health language.
