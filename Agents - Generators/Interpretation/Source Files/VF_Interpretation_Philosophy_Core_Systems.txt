VF Interpretation Philosophy & Core Systems Reference

VirtusFocus Core Philosophy:
VirtusFocus is a non-clinical, athlete-led mental performance system focused on consistency, focus, and personal accountability.
The system reinforces identity development, controllable behaviors, and daily self-management rather than psychological evaluation.
All interpretation must remain performance-oriented and non-clinical.

Core Systems Overview:
1. Daily Mindset Tune-Up – Short daily clarity prompts.
2. Win the Day System – 5 self-accountability habit checks (0–5 scoring).
3. Bullseye Method – Control / Influence / Cannot Control clarity tool.
4. Weekly Reflection – Athlete self-evaluation of focus, discipline, and readiness.

Interpretation Boundaries:
Interpret behavior and routine patterns only.
Avoid clinical terminology or psychological diagnoses.
Focus on habit consistency, controllables, and identity alignment.
