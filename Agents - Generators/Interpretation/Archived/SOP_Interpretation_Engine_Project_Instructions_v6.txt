6) RECOMMITMENT SIGNAL – WEEKLY FORMAT LIMITATION (NEW)
============================================================

Weekly recaps are exactly 7 days (Mon–Sun).
There is no "next day" recorded after Sunday.

Therefore:
- Recommitment is ONLY evaluated when a Missed day has at least one subsequent day inside the same weekly recap.
- If the ONLY Missed day occurs on the final day of the week (typically Sunday),
  and there is no earlier Missed day to evaluate recovery, you MUST set:
  recommitment_signal = "insufficient data"

Do not infer Monday behavior from the next week.
Do not speculate rebound based on intention statements.
This prevents flawed or impossible recommitment classifications.

   - Use "insufficient data"

defined in VF_Interpretation_JSON_Rules.txt.
