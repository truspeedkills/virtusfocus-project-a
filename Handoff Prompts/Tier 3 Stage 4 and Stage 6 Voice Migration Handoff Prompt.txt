3. CONTEXT: WHY THIS WORK MATTERS
============================================================

The pipeline currently has a cohesion problem at the voice
authority level. Stage 3 Coaching Output (athlete-facing
weekly CMs and DDs, Parent CMs) uses Voice Reference and
Voice Foundations as authoritative voice sources.

But:

- Stage 4 (Coach Insights Engine) produces coach-facing
  institutional reports AND parent-facing reports. It
  currently references brand_voice.txt as its voice source.
  The brand_voice.txt file was built for marketing/social
  content, not for coaching reports. This means Stage 4
  output is being generated against the wrong rhetorical
  DNA.

- Stage 6 (Daily Coaching Engine) produces athlete-facing
  daily coaching snippets in the same Coach Arron voice
  used by Stage 3. It currently references brand_voice.txt
  and content_style_guide.txt as its voice sources. Same
  problem: legacy marketing files governing coaching prose.

The Voice Reference document (created in Sessions 96-100)
explicitly includes "Daily Coaching Snippets" in its
purpose statement on line 7. So the Voice Reference is
already designed to govern Stage 6. The migration task
is making Stage 6 actually reference it.

Voice Foundations was built specifically for Stage 3 word
counts and document types. It does not transfer cleanly to
Stage 6 (30-60 word snippets vs 250-450 word CMs) or to
Stage 4 (institutional reports vs athlete coaching). Each
stage needs its own purpose-built foundations doc.

Location: `Agents - Generators\Daily Coaching Engine\Source Files\VF_Daily_Snippet_Voice_Foundations.txt`

Purpose: Pipeline-native brand stance and writing standards
for Stage 6 daily coaching snippets. Lightweight (the
constraints of 30-60 word, 2-3 sentence output are tight
enough that Foundations content should be focused and
short).

Suggested structure:

  Section 1: Purpose and Scope
    - This document is the brand stance for Stage 6 only.
    - Voice Reference is the primary voice authority
      (cross-stage Coach Arron voice).
    - Voice Foundations (Stage 3) is NOT authoritative for
      Stage 6 — daily snippets have different constraints.
    - This document does not duplicate Voice Reference;
      it adds the daily-snippet-specific brand stance.

  Section 2: Brand Stance for Daily Snippets
    - Coach Arron speaking in compressed form
    - Morning bridge: close yesterday, open today
    - Athlete-first (close→open handoff to Tune-Up)
    - Calm authority compressed into 2-3 sentences
    - No hype, no questions, no clinical language
    - Identity-first (the snippet is FROM Coach Arron, TO
      this specific athlete, on this specific morning)

  Section 3: Writing Standards for Daily Snippets
    - 2-3 sentences, 30-60 words, hard cap 3 sentences
    - Close→bridge structure (close yesterday's moment,
      bridge to today's intention)
    - Signed "- Coach Arron"
    - Same em dash ban, contraction default, pipeline
      classification language ban as Stage 3
    - Specific guidance on what to AVOID in compressed
      form (no fragment-form for fragment's sake, no
      cramming of multiple coaching moves into one
      snippet, no recap-style summarization)

  Section 4: Authority Position
    - Voice Reference is the primary voice authority
    - Voice Reference Section 4 Pair 8 (Earn Every
      Sentence applied to compressed form) is the
      relevant exemplar for snippet voice
    - This document is the Stage 6 brand stance layer
    - Daily Snippet Content Model (existing file) is the
      structural specification for what snippets contain
    - Mechanical safety net: same compliance scan protocol
      that exists in v1.3 Project Instructions

  Section 5: Explicit Non-Applicability
    - Long-form rhetorical structures (multi-paragraph
      development, full document opening/middle/close
      arcs) do not apply
    - Daily snippet structure is constrained, not
      free-form
    - The legacy brand_voice.txt and content_style_guide.txt
      marketing files are explicitly NOT authoritative for
      Stage 6 daily snippets

Word target: ~250-400 lines. Should be lighter than Voice
Foundations (which is ~370 lines for Stage 3) because daily
snippet constraints are simpler.
