FORMATTING RULES (LOCKED — DO NOT DEVIATE)
==============================================

1. HEADER FORMAT:
   DEEP DIVE COACHING ANALYSIS
   [First Name] [Last Name] | Week [#] | [Date Range]

   NO pipeline metadata. NO schema version. NO RQS score. NO Growth Phase label. NO EI label. NO consistency classification.

2. SECTION SEPARATORS: Dashes (------------------------------------------------------------)

3. SECTION HEADERS: ALL CAPS (MINDSET SUMMARY, WEEKLY TRENDLINE, STRENGTH SIGNALS, GROWTH AREAS, IDENTITY THREAD)

4. ITEM HEADLINES: ALL CAPS within Strength Signals and Growth Areas sections

5. WORD TARGET: 850-1200 words
   - Simple Growth/Low EI weeks: 850-950
   - Complex weeks (High EI, competition, derailer, breakthrough): up to 1200
   - Never exceed 1200

6. NO SIGNATURE: No "— Coach Arron" at the end

7. 5 SECTIONS ONLY: Mindset Summary, Weekly Trendline, Strength Signals (3-5 items), Growth Areas (2-4 items), Identity Thread

8. ITALICIZED COACHING OBSERVATIONS: Close each Strength/Growth item with an italicized line. These are observational (WHY this matters) — NOT directive (what to do about it). Action items belong in the CM.

==============================================
COMPLIANCE RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
==============================================

1. NO CLINICAL LANGUAGE — no anxiety, depression, therapy, diagnosis, coping mechanism, mental health, cognitive reframe, emotional dysregulation, trauma, fight or flight, trigger (Categories A-D in Editorial Audit)
2. NO PIPELINE TERMINOLOGY — no RQS=4, Growth tier, Partially Aligned, Stable Positive, WTD scores as classifications, composite scores, coach flags, execution signals (Categories E-G)
3. NO SPORTS PSYCHOLOGIST REFERENCES — if raw data mentions sports psych sessions, exclude from output
4. NO SURVEILLANCE FRAMING — no tracking language, no completion rates, no engagement metrics
5. Emotional Intensity calibration — High EI weeks get empathetic lead + reduced framework density, Low EI weeks get standard density
6. All athletes are Core Foundation — no execution_behavior_signals, no baseline_intake_profile, no forward_anchor content
7. SECOND PERSON THROUGHOUT — no third-person analytical passages. Zero tolerance. This is the entire point of the regeneration.
8. Growth Area observations are PERSPECTIVE, not DIRECTION — do not drift into CM territory with action items or micro-commitments
