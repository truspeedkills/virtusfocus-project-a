DIMENSION 2: VOICE PRINCIPLE ALIGNMENT
Assess against the 6 principles from VF_Coach_Arron_Voice_
Reference.txt:

  P0 Service First (does each sentence serve the athlete's
     specific need)
  P1 Start with Meaning, Not Description (lead with why
     it matters)
  P2 Spend Your Words Unevenly (proportional attention)
  P3 Talk Like You've Been in the Room (specific
     behavioral knowledge)
  P4 Earn Every Sentence (no filler)
  P5 React, Don't Compose (responds to the week, not a
     template)

Rate each as STRONG / ADEQUATE / WEAK with a quoted
example demonstrating the rating.

DIMENSION 3: MECHANICAL COMPLIANCE
Binary checks per file (verify with grep where possible):
  - Em dashes: count (target zero in body text)
  - Retired C3 constructions: presence
    ("Here is what," "The version of you," "I also want
    to name," "[N] weeks into this program," "The question
    is [whether]," "Not X. Y." / "not X, but Y," "The
    athletes who [X] are the ones who [Y]")
  - Pipeline classification language: count
    ("Growth weeks," "Win days" as categories, "Gold
    Streak" as label, "Developing phase" as classification,
    etc.)
  - "That is [Noun]." standalone count
  - Contraction register check (formal vs natural)

DIMENSION 4: COACHING AUTHENTICITY (QUALITATIVE)
Rate each CM and DD: STRONG / ADEQUATE / WEAK on:
  A. Does it sound like a real coach who knows this athlete,
     or like an AI processing a JSON?
  B. Is the opening grounded in a specific moment from
     this week, or could it work for any athlete?
  C. Does the coaching move feel reactive to what happened,
     or constructed around the data?
  D. Does the DD add genuine analytical depth beyond the
     CM, or read as expanded version?
  E. Could you identify the athlete and week from the
     output alone?

Quote the strongest passage and the weakest passage in
each file.

DIMENSION 5: EXPRESSIVE RANGE
Note opening approach, sentence rhythm, recognition moves,
and closing moves used. Compare to the prior week (where
applicable) and to the corpus-wide patterns flagged in
prior analyses. Flag any new expressive moves that
weren't present in earlier v2.3 output.
