==============================================
The Weekly Team Snapshot specification has been complete since

The pipeline now has 6 athletes with complete Coach Insights

real signal distributions.
==============================================
CRITICAL CONTEXT — VALIDATION OVERRIDES
==============================================
Two specification constraints must be OVERRIDDEN for this
validation exercise:

OVERRIDE 1 — TEMPORAL ALIGNMENT:
In production, a Weekly Team Snapshot aggregates athletes from
the SAME TEAM during the SAME WEEK. Our 6 validation athletes
span different date ranges:

  Grace Kindel:    Jan 12 – Mar 15, 2026 (7 weeks)
  Tucker Lloyd:    Feb 9 – Mar 15, 2026 (5 weeks)
  Mergim Bushati:  Sep 8, 2025 – Jan 18, 2026 (19 weeks)
  Aidan Burkardt:  Nov 3, 2025 – Feb 22, 2026 (5 weeks)
  Angelo Allen:    Feb 2 – Mar 15, 2026 (4 weeks)
  Jasmine Jones:   May 12 – Jul 13, 2025 (7 weeks)

No single calendar week has all 6 athletes with data. The
maximum overlap is 4 athletes (Feb 9-15 or Feb 16-22, 2026),
which falls below the 5-athlete minimum.

VALIDATION APPROACH: Use each athlete's MOST RECENT WEEK as
the input. This is a cross-temporal validation that tests the
aggregation FORMAT and LOGIC, not temporal accuracy. Label
the output as a "VirtusFocus Program Validation Snapshot"
rather than a real team report. Use today's date range as a
placeholder period.

OVERRIDE 2 — CORE FOUNDATION ATHLETES:
The spec states: "All athletes on a team use the App. Teams
never mix App and Core Foundation athletes." All 6 validation
athletes are CF.

VALIDATION APPROACH: Generate the Team Snapshot using CF data
normally. The execution signal enrichment sections (T1, T2,
T7) will note that execution data is not available — all
composite scores are "insufficient data" and coach_flags are
empty arrays. The base T1-T7 sections work identically
regardless of input_source because they aggregate weekly_tier,
consistency_profile, primary_focus_ring, recommitment_signal,
confidence_momentum, emotional_intensity, growth_phase, and
Composite Readiness — all of which are populated for CF
athletes.

Document both overrides in the validation report. Note what
would change with App athletes (execution enrichment would
populate in T1, T2, T7) and with same-week data (temporal
alignment removes the cross-temporal limitation note).

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS
==============================================
1. HIGH "INSUFFICIENT DATA" IN T4:
   5 of 6 athletes have recommitment = "insufficient data"
   or "Not Applicable." This is normal — these athletes had
   no adverse events requiring recommitment in their most
   recent week. The T4 section should acknowledge this
   gracefully: "The majority of athletes did not experience
   adverse execution events requiring recovery this period"
   rather than "insufficient data across the roster."

2. 100% CENTER-DOMINANT FOCUS (T3):
   All 6 athletes are Center-Dominant. This is a real cohort
   pattern, not an error. The T3 section should note this as
   a positive: strong controllable alignment across the
   roster. In production with 20+ athletes, this distribution
   would likely be more varied.

3. 100% STEADY (YELLOW) COMPOSITE READINESS (T7):
   All 6 athletes are → Steady (Yellow). This is driven by
   the cohort composition: 3 are Moderate stress, 3 have
   Mixed tier, and various factors shift individual readiness
   from Green to Yellow. The T7 section should present this
   neutrally: a steady foundation with no attention flags.

4. MODERATE EI ATHLETES:
   Grace (Wk7), Angelo (Wk4), and Jasmine (Wk7) all have
   Moderate emotional intensity. This maps to T6 as "50%
   Moderate stress." The T6 section should frame this
   neutrally without attributing sources. Do NOT mention
   Jasmine's clinical-risk elements (relationship stress,
   family death, etc.) — T6 presents distributions only.

5. NO POSITIONAL GROUP VARIANTS:
   This validation uses athletes from 5 different sports
   (softball, football, wrestling, track & field, bobsled).
   Positional group variants do not apply. In production, a
   football team with 20+ athletes could generate positional
   group snapshots (e.g., "Offensive Line" with 5+ athletes).

6. JASMINE JONES CLINICAL COMPLIANCE:
   Jasmine's raw data contains 5 clinical-risk elements. None
   of these can appear in the Team Snapshot. Since T1-T7 use
   aggregate distributions only (no individual data), this
   should be inherently safe. Verify that no individual
   narrative content from any athlete leaks into aggregate
   section summaries.
