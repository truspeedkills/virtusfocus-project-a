Key locked decisions (summary — spec has full details):

1. SCORING: 5-tier evidence strength (Strong/Moderate/Weak/
   None/Context Insufficient) per bucket. No numeric 0-100
   scores. Deterministic classification rules using existing
   composite scores, coach flags, and v9.6 fields.

2. CADENCE: Weekly computation. Rolling window across all
   available weeks. Predominant-tier calculation (>=50% of
   evaluable weeks at a tier → that tier overall).

3. THRESHOLD: Progressive. Emerging at 3 weeks (inform-only,
   no Tier 2 change). Established at 5 weeks (full routing).

4. ALIGNMENT SIGNAL: Aligned/Gap/Blind Spot. Top 3 comparison.
   Blind Spot = intake ≤2 AND execution Strong. One Strong
   Blind Spot overrides to overall Blind Spot. 4-step
   classification logic (spec Section D).

5. ROUTING: Tier 2 replacement when Established (execution-
   derived PPD replaces static intake). Tone modification
   across all tiers (Blind Spot=ground, Gap=elevate,
   Aligned=confirm). Zero Stage 4 consumption.

6. JSON: New top-level block `longitudinal_ppd` with status,
   weeks_accumulated, measurement_window_start,
   execution_derived_top_3, execution_derived_all_buckets,
   alignment_signal, core_foundation_fallback (spec Section F).

7. AUDIT: Category F expanded with F6 (alignment labels),
   F7 (longitudinal terminology), F8 (intake-to-execution
   comparison framing). No new criteria — F6-F8 are
   additional terms within existing Criterion 13.

8. BUCKETS: 8 bucket mappings with full evidence tier
   determination rules (spec Section B). Season phase context
   gate for Pressure Reactivity. Ecosystem Friction
   supplemented with content-derived indicators.
