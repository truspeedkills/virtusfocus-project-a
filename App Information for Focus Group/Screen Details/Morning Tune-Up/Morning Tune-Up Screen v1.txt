# Mindset Tune-Up Screen v1 - Mermaid-Ready Specification

## Purpose

Help the athlete mentally **step into the day with intention** and make **one clear commitment** before external demands take over.

This screen is **not motivation**. It is **identity priming + ownership**.

By the time the athlete exits this screen, they should feel: > "I know how I want to show up today - and I've committed to it."

This document translates **Mindset_Tune-Up Screen (v1)** and the rough visual draft into a **precise, linear specification** designed to be converted directly into **Mermaid diagrams**.

## Global Screen Rules

**Structural Rules** - Single vertical flow - No branching paths - No optional exits - One completion outcome

**Behavioral Rules** - Athlete makes commitments, not selections - Progression is earned through acknowledgment - Calm, coach-led pacing

**Mermaid Translation Note** This screen should be diagrammed as a **single linear chain of nodes** with gated progression before completion.

# SCREEN FLOW - TOP → BOTTOM

## 1\. Mindset Statement (Anchor)

### Purpose

Set emotional tone and steady the nervous system.

### UI Rules

- Largest text on screen
- 1-2 short sentences
- Neutral, grounded, coach-like
- No interaction

### Example Copy

"Stay grounded in tough moments. Your steadiness becomes your edge."

### Hard Rules

- ❌ No quotes here
- ❌ No explanations
- ❌ No hype language

## 2\. Focus Word + Quote (Daily Lens)

### Purpose

Give the athlete **one mental filter** for the entire day.

This is a **lens**, not content.

### Layout

- Label: FOCUS WORD
- Focus word in ALL CAPS
- Subtle supporting icon
- Quote beneath in lighter weight

### Interaction

- No action required
- Optional micro-interaction: tap to visually re-highlight focus word (pulse only)

### Example

FOCUS WORD: **STALWART**

"Be sure you put your feet in the right place, then stand firm." - Abraham Lincoln

### Hard Rules

- ❌ Only one focus word
- ❌ No definitions
- ❌ No scrolling quotes

## 3\. Mindset Challenge (Commitment Point)

### Purpose

Convert intention → **action commitment**.

This is the **first active choice of the day** and feeds directly into the Evening Review.

### UI Treatment

- Section label: MINDSET CHALLENGE
- One sentence only
- Specific, actionable behavior

### Interaction

- Single checkbox / toggle
- Required to proceed

**On Tap Behavior** - Subtle check animation - Text weight darkens slightly (affirmed state)

### Example

☐ Hold your composure during one stressful moment today - no reaction.

### Hard Rules

- ❌ Cannot be auto-checked
- ❌ No multiple challenges
- ❌ No vague language ("try", "be better")

## 4\. Quick Wins (Micro-Agreements)

### Purpose

Lower resistance while reinforcing identity reps.

These are **acknowledgments**, not tasks.

### UI Treatment

- Header: QUICK WINS
- Exactly three items
- Each with its own checkbox

### Interaction

- All three must be acknowledged to proceed
- Order does not matter
- Calm visual feedback only (checkmark)

### Example

☐ One deep breath before every drill or moment. ☐ One positive phrase when doubt creeps in. ☐ One decision today that favors growth over comfort.

### Hard Rules

- ❌ No counters
- ❌ No journaling here
- ❌ Not optional

## 5\. Closing Statement (Identity Reinforcement)

### Purpose

Shift the athlete from **doing → being**.

This is emotional glue.

### UI Rules

- Smaller than headline, larger than body text
- 1-2 sentences max
- Identity-based language
- No interaction

### Example

"You don't need to be perfect - you just need to keep showing up like someone who refuses to quit."

### Hard Rules

- ❌ No performance metrics
- ❌ No score language
- ❌ No comparison framing

## 6\. Finish Tune-Up Button (Closure)

### Purpose

Provide psychological closure and mark the rep as complete.

### Button Rules

- Single dominant CTA
- Calm, coach-like language
- Disabled until all required acknowledgments are complete

### Recommended Labels

- Finish Tune-Up (default)
- Lock It In
- I'm Ready

### Interaction

Button becomes enabled only when: - Mindset Challenge is accepted - All Quick Wins are acknowledged

On tap: - Transition to completion state (not directly to Home)

## 7\. Completion State (Micro-Confirmation)

### Purpose

Reinforce: > "You showed up. That counts."

### Behavior

- Brief confirmation message
- Auto-return to Home after 1-2 seconds

### Example

Tune-Up Complete. "Lead with intention today."

### Hard Rules

- ❌ No streak language
- ❌ No score reveal
- ❌ No celebration animation

## Mermaid Diagram Readiness

This screen should map cleanly to:

\[Mindset Tune-Up Start\]  
↓  
\[Mindset Statement\]  
↓  
\[Focus Word + Quote\]  
↓  
\[Mindset Challenge (Required)\]  
↓  
\[Quick Wins (3 Required)\]  
↓  
\[Closing Statement\]  
↓  
\[Finish Tune-Up CTA\]  
↓  
\[Completion State\]  
↓  
\[Return to Home\]

No forks. No loops. No optional exits.

## Why This Screen Works

This screen: - Primes identity - Asks for commitment - Lowers resistance - Creates ownership - Ends calmly

It feels like: > A coach checking in before the day starts - not an app assigning work.
