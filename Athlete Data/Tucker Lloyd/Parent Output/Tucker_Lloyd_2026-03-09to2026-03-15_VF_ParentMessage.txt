WEEKLY CONTEXT

Tucker had an uneven week — his strongest weekday stretch of the program, but the weekend did not carry the same momentum. He is working on building consistency across all seven days, not just the structured ones. That is a normal and important part of the process.

WHAT STOOD OUT

What stood out is how strong Tucker's weekday effort was. He went to the gym twice, handled his classes, managed work, and stayed engaged every single weekday. That level of follow-through shows real capacity when he is locked into a routine.

WHAT'S AHEAD

Spring break begins after this week, which means the daily structure that has been supporting Tucker's strongest days goes away temporarily. The coaching focus is on carrying small, intentional habits into the break — even a couple of deliberate actions can keep the momentum going.

HOW YOU CAN SUPPORT

Encouraging the process and being available if Tucker wants to talk about how things are going is the most helpful support right now. During the break, keeping things normal at home — without managing his routine for him — gives him the space to build that independence on his own terms.
