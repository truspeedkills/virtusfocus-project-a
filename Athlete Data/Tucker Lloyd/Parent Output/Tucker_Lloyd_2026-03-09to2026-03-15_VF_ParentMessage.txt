Tucker had an uneven week — his strongest weekday stretch of the entire program, but the weekend did not carry the same momentum. WHAT'S AHEAD
Spring break starts this week — no classes, no homework, and less structure to anchor the day. The coaching focus is on helping Tucker build some intentional routine through the break, even without the school schedule driving the week.

HOW YOU CAN SUPPORT
