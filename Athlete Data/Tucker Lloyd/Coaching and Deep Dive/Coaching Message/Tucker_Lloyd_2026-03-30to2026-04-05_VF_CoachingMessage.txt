Next week is a normal school week with a normal weekend. No holiday. No family gathering. No fishing trip. That's where the real answer lives.

You picked "Aim Higher Than Yesterday" and said your goal is to "get to Virtus," the first time you've mentioned this program by name as something you're reaching for. Good. Now aim higher than yesterday's habits, not just yesterday's aspirations.
