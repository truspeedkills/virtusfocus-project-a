Monday is the moment of this week. You did the work, you noticed what it added, you named it.

That move tells me you're back in your training environment and the anchor came with you. The 1% better frame is in your training now, and you carried it across without breaking the thread.

You hit that on three days, with Monday's hydration line and Tuesday's "1% better" the cleanest of them. The piece worth getting sharper on this week is widening that pattern across the training days that didn't pick it up. Thursday was a tough upper day with a great chest pump, and that's all your recap says about it. Saturday and Sunday were family days, which is what they were and what they should be. Training days are where the pattern needs to widen.
