  After each practice, identify one specific thing you did well. "Got
  better" becomes "scored from the underhook twice." Specificity
  sharpens awareness.

• MANAGE TRAINING VOLUME
  Three consecutive high-intensity weeks. Build in intentional recovery
  to prevent physical burnout. Rest with purpose.

• SUSTAIN WEEKEND STANDARD
  This week proved Saturday and Sunday can be Win days. Make that the
  expectation, not the exception.

• MICRO-COMMITMENT: During live wrestling this week, set a specific
  first-attack goal for each go — pick one setup and commit to executing
  it within the first 30 seconds without waiting for the "right moment."
  Target: initiated chosen attack within 30 seconds on at least 3 of 5
  live goes.
