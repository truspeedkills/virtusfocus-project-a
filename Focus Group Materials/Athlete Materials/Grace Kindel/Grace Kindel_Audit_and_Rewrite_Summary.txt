AUDIT LESSONS (CARRIED FORWARD)

1. Self-audit catch during Wk1 DD draft: "version of confidence"
   appeared 4 times in first draft despite explicit awareness of
   Pair 7 retirement. The construction is sticky because it reads
   as natural English when describing developmental forms of a
   trait. Mitigation: substitute "kind of [X]" or anchor to
   specific behavior.

2. Self-audit catch during Wk7 CM draft: "Not X. Not Y. Not Z."
   appeared in YOUR ACTION instructions section as a directive
   list of exclusions. Different function from rhetorical opening
   pattern but still triggers 14.10 detection. Mitigation: replace
   with single-sentence list using commas.

3. Self-audit catch during Wk7 DD draft: "Coaches notice the
   athletes who [X]" reads as a softened version of the retired
   "The athletes who [X] are the ones who [Y]." Even softened
   formulations should redirect to specific-athlete framing.

4. Wk1 DD initial draft sat below the 850-word floor at ~740.
   First-week DDs with no prior data are naturally thinner because
   the trendline section has no longitudinal material to draw on.
   Resolved by adding depth via unutilized JSON data (Tuesday
   mindset_resonance and athlete_voice.achievement_statement
   "earned-belonging" thread).

5. Wk7 DD initial draft exceeded 1200-word cap at 1273. Trimmed
   primarily from Growth Areas section by removing scaffolding
   sentences without losing analytical depth.

6. Coach Insights v1.5/v1.9 institutional voice has held quality
   standards across versions. No rewrites needed for either week.
   This suggests Stage 4 voice authority migration completed in
   Session 104 (purpose-built VF_Coach_Insights_Output_Voice_
   Standards.txt) is producing stable institutional output.
