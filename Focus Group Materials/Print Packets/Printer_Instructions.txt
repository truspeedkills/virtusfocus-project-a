NOTES ON COVER STOCK

Cover stock pages are the artifact packet cover pages within
each combined packet. They are intended to print on segment-
colored cover stock to provide a visual divider between the
worksheet and the artifact contents, and to support at-a-
glance segment sorting during the breakout transition.

Worksheet pages (pages 1 through 7 of each packet) print on
standard white paper. The worksheet design has segment-colored
header bands baked into the layout, so colored paper stock is
not required for those pages.

All artifact pages after the cover (athlete snapshots, coaching
messages, deep dives, coach insights, parent materials, team
snapshot, mockup printouts) print on standard white paper.


COLOR REQUIREMENTS

Color printing is required for the entire packet. Several pages
contain colored elements that lose meaning if printed in
grayscale: gradient progression bars on the athlete progression
visuals, amber-bordered callout boxes on coaching message
cards, traffic-light color bands on the team snapshot, and
header bands on every worksheet page. Standard CMYK four-
color process is sufficient.
